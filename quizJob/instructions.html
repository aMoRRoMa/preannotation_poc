Overview

In this job, you will be presented with tweets about American Airlines. Review the tweets to determine the relevance to the subject and the overall sentiments of the tweet. 

Steps

  1. Read the tweet.
  2. Determine if the tweet mentions the company's Twitter account -- American Airlines (@AmericanAir)
  3. Determine if the tweet is positive, neutral, or negative.
  4. If the tweet is negative, you will be presented with a list of common complaints.
    • Choose as many complaint categories as apply to the tweet. 

Rules & Tips

The posts can be classified as positive, negative or neutral: 

  • Positive means some aspects of the tweet uncover a positive mood, such as praise, recommendations or a favorable comparison.
  • Neutral means that the post is only informative in nature and provides no hint as to the mood of the author. 
  • Negative means some aspects of the tweet uncover a negative mood such as, criticism, insults or a negative comparison.
